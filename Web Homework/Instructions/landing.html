Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this assignment is to look into weather data that, specifically understanding how certain measurements change as you get closer to the equator. As you will notice, the temperature tends to be at its peak when at the equator (or a latitude of 0). Conversely, the temperature goes down as you get further from the equator. The way the analysis was done is that we took matplotlib to plot cloudiness, temperature, wind speed, and humidity vs. latitude.

Ultimately, living in certain parts of the world has some drawbacks, and it isn't possible to find the perfect place to live, even as much as you may want to.
